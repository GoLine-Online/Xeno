    FAQs Uncovered

    Get answers to your burning questions about XenoPoints and start optimizing your music streaming experience today.

    How do I earn points?

    Earn points by inviting Xeno Music to new servers. Each successful invitation rewards you with XenoPoints.

    What can I redeem points for?

    Redeem your points for exclusive access, premium content, and other specials on the Xeno Music.

    How many invites equal points?

    Each successful invite to a new server will earn you 100 points. The more you invite, the more you earn!

    Are points transferable?

    No, points are not transferable and are linked to your individual Xeno Music account.
